Purpose

The purpose of this website is to Combine consumer's love of water sports with the thrill of whitewater, rafting offers single or multi-day experiences for families; and the possibility of significant profits for entrepreneurs throughout Botswana

Audience

The target audience for this rafting website typically consists of adventure enthusiasts and thrill-seekers who crave exhilarating experiences in the great outdoors. These individuals are usually between the ages of 18 and 45, physically active, and enjoy challenging activities
